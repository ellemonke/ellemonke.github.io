Ellen Hsu

Hi, my name is Ellen Hsu. I'm an ambitious, driven individual with over 15 years experience in marketing, particularly digital. For the past 5 years, I’ve owned a successful retail shop in downtown Portland called Vintalier. So I have an in-depth understanding of brand strategy, multi-channel marketing, merchandising and consumer retail. I’m a very strategic and creative thinker so I like to tackle the tough problems and turn them into efficient, nicely packaged products.

Vintalier first opened in 2013 as a vintage clothing store in the Pearl District of Portland, Oregon. It soon evolved into carrying new clothing with a focus on cool and casual styles for busy modern women. In 2017, Vintalier moved to the West End of downtown Portland. Vintalier is now a lifestyle store with simple, functional, and beautiful clothes, accessories and home décor to pamper oneself with.
